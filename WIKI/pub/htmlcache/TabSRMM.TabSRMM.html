All pages about TabSRMM

All pages related to the messaging plugin for Miranda IM. Over the years, tabSRMM has evolved to a quite complex piece of software, offering many features and options. This is an attempt to document them in a fairly detailed way, but also providing easy to understand information for new users.

This is always under heavy construction. Pages are changing frequently and new stuff is added on a regular base.
